===== SHIPPING & RETURN POLICY =====
Last Updated: 2026-03-01

--- SHIPPING POLICY ---

1. Domestic Shipping (Continental US):
   - Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days, FREE on orders over $50
   - Standard Shipping (under $50): 5-7 business days, $5.99 flat rate
   - Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days, $12.99
   - Next-Day Shipping: 1 business day, $24.99 (order by 2:00 PM EST)
   - Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.

2. Alaska & Hawaii:
   - Standard Shipping: 7-14 business days, $9.99
   - Expedited Shipping: 3-5 business days, $19.99
   - Next-Day Shipping: Not available

3. International Shipping:
   - Canada: 7-14 business days, $14.99
   - Europe: 10-21 business days, $24.99
   - Asia-Pacific: 14-28 business days, $29.99
   - Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the buyer.
   - International orders are not eligible for free shipping.

4. Order Tracking:
   - A tracking number is emailed within 24 hours of shipment.
   - Track your order at: https://www.example.com/track
   - For tracking issues, contact support@example.com.

5. Shipping Restrictions:
   - We do not ship to PO Boxes for items over 10 lbs.
   - Some electronics may have export restrictions for certain countries.

--- RETURN POLICY ---

1. Standard Return Window:
   - You may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
   - Items must be unused, in original packaging, with all accessories included.
   - Return shipping is FREE for defective or incorrect items.
   - For non-defective returns, a $7.99 return shipping fee will be deducted from the refund.

2. Extended Holiday Returns:
   - Orders placed between November 1 and December 31 may be returned until January 31.

3. Non-Returnable Items:
   - Opened air purifier filters (for hygiene reasons)
   - Personalized or custom-engraved products
   - Gift cards
   - Items marked "Final Sale"

4. How to Initiate a Return:
   Step 1: Log into your account at https://www.example.com/returns
   Step 2: Select the order and items you wish to return
   Step 3: Choose a return reason
   Step 4: Print the prepaid return label (if eligible)
   Step 5: Ship the item within 7 days of initiating the return

5. Refund Processing:
   - Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
   - Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
   - If you paid with a credit card, allow 1-2 billing cycles for the credit to appear.

6. Exchanges:
   - We do not process direct exchanges.
   - Please return the original item and place a new order.

7. Damaged or Defective Items:
   - Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage.
   - Email: support@example.com or call 1-800-555-0199 (Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM EST)
   - We will ship a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund.

--- WARRANTY CLAIMS ---

For warranty-related issues (beyond the 30-day return window), please contact:
   - Email: warranty@example.com
   - Phone: 1-800-555-0199
   - Include your order number and a description of the issue.
   - Warranty periods vary by product (see product listing for details).
