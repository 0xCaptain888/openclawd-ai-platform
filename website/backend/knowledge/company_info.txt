--- COMPANY OVERVIEW ---

NovaTech Solutions was founded in 2019 by a team of engineers and designers who were frustrated by the fragmented smart home ecosystem. Our first product, the SmartHome Hub Pro, was born from the idea that you should not need 10 different apps to control your home.

Since then, we have expanded into office ergonomics, personal electronics, and home wellness products. Every product we design follows three principles:

1. Interoperability: Our products work together and with 500+ third-party brands.
2. Sustainability: We use recycled materials, minimal packaging, and energy-efficient designs.
3. Simplicity: Setup should take minutes, not hours. No technical expertise required.
